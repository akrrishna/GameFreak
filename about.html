About GameFreak

Driven by passion, we are a global leader in digital interactive entertainment.

Through our cutting-edge games, innovative services, and powerful technologies, we bring worlds with infinite possibilities to more than 580 million active players and fans around the globe, and are recognized for a portfolio of critically acclaimed, high-quality brands such as GF SPORTS™ FIFA, Battlefield™, Apex Legends™, The Sims™, Madden™ NFL, Need for Speed™, Titanfall™ and F1™.
